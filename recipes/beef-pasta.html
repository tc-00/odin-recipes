Creamy Tomato Beef Pasta Recipe

Home

Description

This creamy tomato beef pasta is a one-pot dish that combines ground beef, pasta, and a rich tomato sauce with cream. It's a comforting and satisfying meal that's easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves (finely minced)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 500g beef mince
  • 2 tsp italian herbs
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 0.5 tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp black pepper
  • 1 litre chicken stock/broth
  • 350g pasta
  • 180ml double cream

For Serving

  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
