Smash Burgers Recipe

Home

Description

Smash burgers are a type of hamburger that is made by smashing a ball of ground beef onto a hot griddle or skillet, creating a crispy, caramelized crust. They are typically served on a soft bun with various toppings.

Ingredients

  • 4 brioche burger buns
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g/1lb ground beef (15-20% fat)
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 red onion
  • iceberg lettuce
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 gherkin
  • ketchup, mayonaise, mustard, or sauce of your preference
